# LUXOR Campaign Prompts — Batch 1

## Prompt 1: The Algorithmic Curator
**Params:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --s 250 --v 6.1`

SUBJECT: A gender-neutral professional, mid-30s, standing in a relaxed power stance. Natural skin with visible pores, realistic micro-texture, slight asymmetry in the jaw, matte finish. Wearing a perfectly cut oversized blazer and wide-leg trousers.

MEDIUM: Hyperrealistic editorial fashion photography. Full-frame sensor, 85mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, crisp optical clarity, true-to-life color calibration. Volumetric micro-shadowing and realistic light scattering on skin and fabric.

ENVIRONMENT: Minimalist brutalist interior with polished concrete walls and a large frosted glass window. Beside the subject, a dynamic floating holographic UI grid (Luxor app interface) displays the current outfit breakdown, with glowing cyan lines tagging the blazer, trousers, and shoes.

LIGHTING: Soft, diffused north light entering through the frosted window, wrapping around the subject's form. The holographic interface casts a faint, focused volumetric cyan fill light onto the subject's left side.

COLOR: Desaturated luxury palette — warm greige, soft ash grey, pure white. Accents of deep charcoal and saturated cyan from the UI. Skin tones rendered with true-to-life color calibration.

MOOD: Calm, powerful, algorithmically precise. The convergence of high-end wardrobe curation and seamless AI integration.

COMPOSITION: Subject positioned in the left third of the frame, facing slightly right toward the floating UI grid which occupies the right half. Vertical window mullions and concrete panel lines create strong structural geometry. Shallow depth of field blurs the background, keeping focus on the subject and the holographic tags.

## Prompt 2: The Time Dividend (Before/After)
**Params:** `--ar 16:9 --s 200 --v 6.1`

SUBJECT: A woman, early 30s. Natural skin with visible pores, realistic micro-texture, subtle redness around the nose and dark circles under eyes, matte finish. LEFT: stressed, tired expression, messy hair. RIGHT: same subject, clear eyes, relaxed mouth, calm composure.

MEDIUM: Split screen diptych. Hyperrealistic editorial photography. Full-frame sensor, 85mm lens for both panels. Shallow depth of field, crisp optical clarity, volumetric micro-shadowing.

ENVIRONMENT: LEFT: chaotic morning closet — clothes spilling over a chair, open drawers, messy pile of shoes. RIGHT: same person in a stark white minimalist space, a single curated outfit hanging on a sleek wooden hanger.

LIGHTING: LEFT: harsh tungsten overhead light (3000K), creating heavy shadows on the face and emphasizing texture. RIGHT: large softbox cool north light (5500K), wrapping the subject and outfit in soft, flattering light.

COLOR: LEFT: warm, cluttered, desaturated browns and yellows, deep shadows. RIGHT: cool, clean, slate blue, crisp beige, pure white. True-to-life color calibration.

MOOD: Transformation. Relief. The visceral removal of cognitive overhead. Pure problem/solution storytelling.

COMPOSITION: Diptych layout. Luxor logo perfectly centered between the two panels. The subject is mirrored in position in both frames, directly addressing the camera. The stark contrast in expression, lighting, and environment drives the narrative.

## Prompt 3: The Infinite Wardrobe
**Params:** `--ar 16:9 --style raw --s 50 --v 6.1`

SUBJECT: A woman, late 20s, standing in profile, one hand resting on a garment on a central chrome rack. Natural skin with visible pores, realistic micro-texture, faint freckles, matte finish.

MEDIUM: Hyperrealistic architectural/interior photography. Architectural Digest editorial style. Full-frame sensor, 85mm lens, crisp optical clarity. Volumetric micro-shadowing, realistic light scattering.

ENVIRONMENT: Stark minimalist architectural closet. White polished concrete, seamless. Garments float in glowing holographic UI slots numbered 1 to 15 (Luxor digital wardrobe interface). A single physical chrome clothing rack stands in the exact center, connecting the real to the infinite digital grid.

LIGHTING: Ambient cove lighting from ceiling recesses, creating soft geometric shadows. Each holographic garment slot emits a faint volumetric white/cyan glow. The physical garment catches the light with realistic specular highlights.

COLOR: Monochrome with a digital edge — pure white, deep charcoal, chrome silver. The glowing cyan UI slots provide the only saturated color, reinforcing the futuristic aesthetic.

MOOD: Futuristic serenity. Boundless capacity. The infinite wardrobe, organized and accessible. Physical and digital in perfect symmetry.

COMPOSITION: Symmetrical vertical composition. The chrome rack is dead center. The holographic slots form a perfect grid behind it, creating strong depth. The subject's silhouette integrates the human element into the clean, infinite digital architecture.
